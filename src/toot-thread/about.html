Enter the url of a toot and press Submit, and the app will progressively walk back through the thread to find the parent until there are no more.

You can append a toot parameter with a url encoding to pre-fill the form. This will look a bit like this:

...?toot=https%3A%2F%2Fmastodon.me.uk%2F%40garius%2F110340128553620052

Caveats:

  • When you submit the form, the window is updated. You can then bookmark or share that link.
  • This only works on public toots.
  • There is a delay in fetching the parent toot.
